# SellCraft AI — Project Overview

## One-Line Pitch

The AI-powered listing intelligence platform that tells Indian marketplace sellers exactly what to write, why it works, and how to rank higher — across every Indian marketplace.

## The Problem

Indian marketplace sellers lose 20-40% of potential revenue because their listings are unoptimized. The knowledge to fix this is locked in expensive agencies, experienced sellers, and opaque platform algorithms. Current AI tools are built for Western markets and fundamentally misunderstand Indian commerce behavior.

## The Solution

A platform that reverse-engineers what sells on each Indian marketplace and generates listings that are algorithmically optimized for that specific platform, category, price point, and buyer persona. Not generic AI writing — data-driven marketplace intelligence.

## Why Now

1. AI capabilities have matured enough to generate commerce-grade content
2. Quick commerce (Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy) is creating millions of new sellers who need speed
3. ONDC is democratizing commerce — new sellers entering who need guidance
4. India's e-commerce GMV growing 25-30% YoY — optimization is no longer optional
5. Competition on marketplaces is intensifying — listing quality directly impacts survival

## Category We're Creating

**"Seller Intelligence Platform"** — not a copywriting tool, not a listing generator, not a marketplace plugin. A new category that combines AI generation + marketplace intelligence + performance optimization.

## Competitive Positioning

We are NOT competing with Jasper, Copy.ai, or ChatGPT.
We are competing with the seller's current workflow: copying competitors, guessing keywords, and hoping for the best.

Our real competitors are:
- The seller doing it manually (biggest segment)
- Marketplace agencies (expensive, doesn't scale)
- Generic AI tools used with bad prompts (poor output quality)
- Platform-provided tools (basic, non-differentiated)
