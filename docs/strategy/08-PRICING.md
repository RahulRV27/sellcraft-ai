# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

**Indian SMB pricing rule:** Price must be low enough that the decision is a no-brainer, but high enough that users take the product seriously. Free tools in India are perceived as low-quality. Tools priced at ₹999/month hit the "worth trying" sweet spot.

**Value-based anchoring:** We don't sell "AI listings." We sell "revenue improvement." If our tool helps a seller increase monthly revenue by ₹10,000, a ₹999/month price is a 10x ROI.

## Pricing Rationale

### Why These Price Points

| Plan | Price | Logic |
|------|-------|-------|
| Free | ₹0 | Acquisition. Must be genuinely useful to drive word-of-mouth. |
| Pro | ₹999 | Below ₹1,000 psychological barrier. Similar to Netflix/Spotify pricing in India. Affordable for any seller doing ₹50K+/month GMV. |
| Business | ₹2,999 | 3x Pro price for 4x features. Sweet spot for sellers doing ₹2-5L/month GMV. ROI is obvious. |
| Enterprise | ₹9,999 | For sellers/brands doing ₹10L+/month. The cost is irrelevant vs the value. |
| Agency | ₹24,999 | Per-agency pricing. Averages to ₹500/client at 50 clients. Agencies charge clients ₹15-50K, so this is a fraction. |

- Diwali Special: 40% off annual plans (October)

- Loss leader that feeds the data flywheel

### 3. Annual Commitment Reward
- 2 months free on annual plans (standard)
- Pay annually, get one free listing audit per month

### 4. Startup / Student Discount
- 50% off for registered startups (Startup India)
- Free plan with higher limits for students learning e-commerce
