# Customer Personas

## Persona 1: Raju — The Marketplace Reseller

### Profile
- **Location:** Surat / Jaipur / Ludhiana (Tier 2 cities)
- **Age:** 25-40
- **Tech comfort:** WhatsApp fluent, uses smartphone for everything, limited laptop use
- **Business:** Resells textiles/fashion/accessories on Meesho + Amazon
- **SKU count:** 50-200 products
- **Monthly GMV:** ₹2-10 lakhs
- **Team:** Solo or 1-2 helpers

### Behavior
- Copies competitor listings (literally copy-paste)
- Writes in broken English or basic Hindi
- Learns from YouTube videos and WhatsApp seller groups
- Price is the #1 concern in every decision
- Uploads product photos from phone camera
- Doesn't understand keywords or SEO
- Checks marketplace seller app 10+ times/day

### Pain Points
- "I don't know what to write in the description"
- "My products are the same as competitors but they sell more"
- "I don't have time to write good listings for 200 products"
- "I can't afford to hire someone for this"
- "I tried ChatGPT but the output doesn't look right for my products"

### What They Need from Us
- Paste product details → get ready-to-use listing in 10 seconds
- Hindi/Hinglish interface option
- WhatsApp-based workflow (send photo + details, get listing back)
- ₹299-499/month pricing
- Category-specific templates (fashion, electronics, home)
- Bulk listing generation for multiple products

### Acquisition Channel
- WhatsApp seller groups (organic + paid promotions)
- YouTube tutorials on marketplace selling
- Referrals from other sellers
- Free listing audits as lead magnets

### Willingness to Pay
- ₹299-999/month (very price-sensitive)
- Will pay if they see direct revenue increase
- Needs free trial that delivers immediate value
- Will churn quickly if they don't see results in 2 weeks

## Persona 2: Priya — The D2C Brand Manager

### Profile
- **Location:** Mumbai / Bangalore / Delhi (Tier 1 cities)
- **Age:** 28-38
- **Tech comfort:** High, uses multiple SaaS tools daily
- **Business:** D2C brand (skincare/food/lifestyle) expanding to marketplaces
- **SKU count:** 100-1,000 products
- **Monthly GMV:** ₹10-50 lakhs per marketplace
- **Team:** 3-10 people in e-commerce team

### Behavior
- Has brand guidelines that must be followed
- Sells on 3-5 marketplaces simultaneously
- Runs ads on marketplace platforms
- Tracks analytics and conversion rates
- Has seasonal catalog updates (festivals, new launches)
- Compares performance across platforms
- Works with an agency or has in-house team

### Pain Points
- "We need consistent brand voice across all marketplaces"
- "Adapting listings for each platform is incredibly time-consuming"
- "Our A+ content doesn't convert as well as competitors'"
- "We don't know which keywords our competitors are ranking for"
- "Festival season requires updating hundreds of listings quickly"

### What They Need from Us
- Multi-platform listing generation with brand voice consistency
- A+ / Enhanced Brand Content generator
- Competitor keyword analysis
- Bulk optimization across platforms
- Seasonal listing update automation
- Brand tone customization
- Team collaboration features

### Acquisition Channel
- LinkedIn (D2C founder communities)
- D2C events and conferences
- Content marketing (blog, newsletter)
- Direct outreach
- Partner channel (Shopify ecosystem, agency referrals)

### Willingness to Pay
- ₹2,999-9,999/month
- Evaluates based on ROI and time saved
- Longer evaluation period (2-4 weeks)
- Values features, integrations, and support quality
- Lower churn once onboarded (stickier workflows)

## Persona 3: Vikram — The E-commerce Agency

### Profile
- **Location:** Metro cities
- **Age:** 30-45
- **Tech comfort:** Very high
- **Business:** Manages marketplace accounts for 20-100+ brands
- **Team:** 5-30 people

### Behavior
- Manages listings at scale (thousands of SKUs across clients)
- Charges clients monthly retainers (₹15K-1L per client)
- Needs to show measurable improvement to retain clients
- Uses multiple tools (Helium 10, advertising tools, analytics)
- Hires junior team members for listing work
- Always looking for efficiency gains

### Pain Points
- "My team spends 60% of their time on listing creation and optimization"
- "Maintaining quality across 50 client accounts is impossible"
- "I need to show clients measurable improvement in listing quality"
- "Every platform has different requirements — training new hires takes months"
- "I can't scale my agency beyond 50 clients with current processes"

### What They Need from Us
- Multi-account management (separate workspaces per client)
- White-label option (brand it as their own tool)
- Bulk operations (optimize 1000 listings in one click)
- Listing quality reports (PDF exports for client presentations)
- Team member access controls
- API access for custom integrations
- Priority support and dedicated account manager

### Acquisition Channel
- Direct sales (outbound + inbound)
- Agency partnership program
- Agency community events
- Case study marketing
- Referral incentives

### Willingness to Pay
- ₹14,999-49,999/month (scales with number of accounts)
- Per-account pricing model works well
- Values time savings and scalability over cost
- Longest sales cycle (1-3 months) but highest LTV
- Very low churn once integrated into workflows

## Persona 4: Arjun — The Quick Commerce Brand

### Profile
- **Location:** Metro cities
- **Age:** 28-40
- **Tech comfort:** High
- **Business:** FMCG/grocery brand selling on Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart
- **SKU count:** 20-200 products
- **Monthly GMV:** ₹5-30 lakhs per platform

### Behavior
- Operates in a speed-first environment
- Compliance and catalog accuracy are critical (wrong info = deactivation)
- Product attributes matter more than descriptions (weight, size, MRP)
- Search terms are short and functional ("atta 5kg", "milk 500ml")
- New product onboarding speed is a competitive advantage
- Tracks real-time performance metrics

### Pain Points
- "Getting catalog attributes right for each quick commerce platform is tedious"
- "Each platform has different attribute requirements"
- "We need to onboard 50 new SKUs this week and can't afford errors"
- "Search optimization on quick commerce is different from Amazon"
- "Compliance rejections slow down our launches"

### What They Need from Us
- Quick commerce specific attribute optimization
- Compliance checker (flag issues before submission)
- Rapid SKU onboarding (upload product sheet → get platform-ready listings)
- Platform-specific search optimization (Blinkit ≠ Zepto ≠ Swiggy)
- Category-specific templates for FMCG/grocery
- Bulk attribute filling from product images/packaging

### Acquisition Channel
- Quick commerce brand communities
- FMCG industry events
- LinkedIn outreach to brand managers
- Content marketing on quick commerce optimization

### Willingness to Pay
- ₹1,999-4,999/month
- Speed of onboarding is the primary value driver
- Compliance accuracy justifies the cost
- Medium sales cycle (2-4 weeks)

## Persona Prioritization for MVP

| Priority | Persona | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| P0 | Priya (D2C Brand) | Highest ARPU, clearest ROI, fastest to convert, best reference customers |
| P1 | Raju (Reseller) | Largest volume, drives viral growth, proves mass-market viability |
| P2 | Vikram (Agency) | Highest LTV, multiplier effect, but longer sales cycle |
| P3 | Arjun (Quick Commerce) | Growing fast but different product requirements — Phase 2 |

**Key Insight:** Launch targeting Priya (D2C brands) for revenue, then Raju (resellers) for volume. Agencies come through organic pull once product-market fit is proven.
